Frequently asked questions

  • How much does liposuction cost in NYC?

    Liposuction in NYC typically costs $3,500–$9,500 per treatment area for surgeon fees. Total cost depends on areas treated, technique, and anesthesia type. A single area with local anesthesia may total $4,000–$7,000 all-in; multiple areas under general anesthesia can range from $15,000–$30,000+.

  • What factors affect the cost of liposuction?

    The main factors are: number of treatment areas, technique (VASER and BodyTite cost more), anesthesia type (local ~$500, general $2,500–$3,000), facility fees, and surgeon experience. NYC board-certified surgeons typically charge 20–40% more than the national average.

  • What is VASER liposuction and does it cost more?

    VASER uses ultrasound energy to liquefy fat before removal, allowing more precise contouring with less trauma. It typically costs $1,500–$2,500 more per area than standard lipo. Dr. Smith uses VASER for high-definition results and fibrous areas.

  • Are anesthesia and facility fees included in the estimate?

    No — estimates shown are surgeon fees only. Anesthesia and facility fees add approximately $500 for a single area under local anesthesia, up to $1,500–$3,000 for multiple areas under general anesthesia. Exact fees are confirmed at your consultation.

  • Is financing available?

    Yes. Dr. Smith's practice offers financing through CareCredit and Alphaeon Credit, allowing patients to spread costs over 12–60 months. Financing details are discussed at consultation.
